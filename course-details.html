Fundamentals of nuclear chemistry

Karthik

Nuclear chemistry is a branch of chemistry that deals with the study of the atomic nucleus, which is the central part of an atom that contains protons and neutrons.

Course objectives

  • Learn about the processes involved in nuclear reactions
  • Explore how nuclear chemistry contributes to energy generation
  • Understand the principles of radiation protection and safety measures
